Join the Collective

Become part of a new form of union for software developers. Work on projects you choose, share in collective revenue, and participate in democratic decision-making.

Why Join?

Proportional Revenue Sharing

Senior members can choose to receive a proportional share of all collective revenue from contracts, software sales, and other income streams. Your contribution directly benefits you.

Complete Transparency

All budgets, salaries, and financial decisions are completely public. You'll always know how the collective operates and where resources are allocated.

Democratic Decision-Making

Have a real voice in how the collective operates. Major decisions require democratic approval, ensuring your interests are represented.

Job Security

Protection from arbitrary layoffs or firings. The collective functions as a union of workers, prioritizing member security and stability.

Autonomy & Flexibility

Work on projects that match your skills and interests. Organize yourself into project groups and contribute what you do best.

Community Support

Join a supportive environment where members grow and thrive together. Access collective resources, spaces, and community events.

Membership Path

1

Apply to Join

Submit an application expressing your interest in joining the collective. Senior members will review your application and setup an interview if necessary.

2

Junior Membership

Juniors may receieve salaried pay or reduced revenue sharing, contingent on budget approval. Juniors are expected to complete the following requirements: remain 1 year at the collective, contribute to multiple projects, learn new skills, and lead a project to release.

3

Apply for Senior Membership

After meeting the requirements, juniors may apply for senior membership with a 2/3 vote approval.

4

Senior Membership

Seniors have the option for revenue sharing or salaried pay, contingent on budget approval. Seniors are expected to both contribute and lead projects, move around to fill important roles for any work within the collective, and contribute to the development of juniors.

How to Apply

To apply for membership, please reach out to us through our contact channels. A senior member will review your application and discuss the next steps. We're looking for developers who are serious about committing their work to the collective and who share our vision of a more equitable software industry.
